About Vikas Medicare

Vikas Medicare was established on October 22, 1995, by Mr. Vikas Narang on his father's birthday. What started as a humble venture has now transformed into one of Prayagraj's most trusted wholesale medicine suppliers.

1995

Vikas Narang established Vikas Medicare on October 22nd, 1995, as a tribute to his father on his birthday. The business began with a small inventory and limited pharmaceutical connections.

2000s

Within five years, Vikas Medicare expanded its operations and established strong relationships with leading pharmaceutical manufacturers, creating a robust supply chain across Prayagraj.

2010s

The business modernized its inventory management systems and expanded its distribution network to cover the entire Prayagraj region, becoming a key player in the local pharmaceutical supply industry.

Present Day

Today, with 30 years of experience, Vikas Medicare stands as a pillar of reliability and quality in Prayagraj's pharmaceutical wholesale market, serving hundreds of medical stores and healthcare facilities.
